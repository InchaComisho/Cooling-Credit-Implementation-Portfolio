## Overview

Cooling Credits are not a single technology. They are an institutional framework that connects urban cooling, ocean circulation, soil regeneration, forest regeneration, organic resource circulation, mobility cooling, water-circulation cities, and Civilization OS.

The following links represent related concepts that may become evaluation targets, application targets, or theoretical foundations for Cooling Credits.

This page shows that Cooling Credits are not an isolated idea, but an integrated system already connected to multiple implementation candidates, theoretical foundations, urban models, ocean models, soil-regeneration models, mobility technologies, and civilizational design frameworks.

## Conclusion

This link system shows that Cooling Credits are not an isolated idea. They are an integrated framework already connected to multiple implementation candidates, theoretical foundations, urban models, ocean models, soil-regeneration models, mobility technologies, and civilizational design systems.

Cooling Credits allow companies, municipalities, farmers, researchers, product manufacturers, urban designers, ocean engineers, and energy businesses to participate in planetary cooling from their own fields.

A completely new magical technology is not required. What is required is a framework that integrates, measures, evaluates, and implements existing technologies, natural cooling principles, water cycles, soils, plants, microorganisms, oceans, urban design, and mobility technologies.

That framework is Cooling Credits.
